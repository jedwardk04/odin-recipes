Chicken Tacos

These delicious tacos use chicken as the main source of meat. One can use the standard of cheese and lettuce, and feel free to add any other part of the typical taco. Could be tomatoes, onions, salsa, sour cream, hot sauce, and more!

    Ingredients
  • tortilla
  • shredded chicken
  • diced tomatoes
  • shredded lettuce
  • salsa
